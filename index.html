About Us

Titan is a well trusted family business. We are a modern, creative, forward thinking company that believes in 100% customer satisfaction and making you feel part of the team and integrating you in the project from start to finish. We are a small family run company that believes in Quality not Quantity. We don't take on many various jobs simultaneously to assure focus, perfection and a speedy work rate to get the job done quickly. We offer only the finest landscaping, drive and pathway products with second to none customer service.  We are dedicated to making your landscaping and driveway dreams a reality.

With Titan your design possibilities are virtually limitless, we only use the best materials and our dedicated team have years of experience and can help guide you in  choosing the best setup possible. We can improve the character and value of your home for less money than you think with a prestigious resin bound, block paved drive and patio. A full range of traditional and modern colours and designs are available. A complete service is provided with site preparation if necessary. We can provide Decorative Walling, Creative Paving, Slabbing as well as Blockwork and Graveling. Titan also offers a Repairing service to your driveway and its not as complicated and costly as you might think. We can repair your driveway in many ways, including a full resurface, re-levelling an uneven surface, fix pot-holes, relay loose bricks and revive tired looking paving. No matter what it is that needs doing, we are sure we can assist you.

We work closely with our customers in order to guarantee complete satisfaction and peace of mind. No job is too big or too small, with our expertise ensuring that each project is properly organised and efficiently completed to the highest possible standard. As Titan is a family run business you will always find we have time to answer any questions and you will be talking to a family member not an operator. We are very competitive and will beat most quotes. Why not give us a call or contact us via the website and you will be pleasantly surprised.
